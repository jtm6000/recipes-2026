     
Pasta you can do literally anytime

Ingredients:
- Angel Hair / Linguini (apex noodle pasta)
- Olive oil
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Can of Clams
- White Wine
- crushed red pepper

Instructions:
- boil pasta to package time instructions (use lower number or lower number -1, you need al dente)
- Oil in pan
- Garlic like a minute barely
- add clams/juice/white wine (healthy splash)
- boil off wine
- when pasta done, tong it from pot to pan, this keeps starchy pasta water
- heat off, stir in pan until thickened
- crushed red pepper
